About Us

We are a small upcoming company from Harare, Zimbabwe. Our main goal is to be a hub that foster creativity, innovation growth and dreams. We seek to infiltrate the market by capturing the dreams of many small to medium enterprises and turning them into a reality. "Dreams, Goals and Wishes" is where our business mindset is based.
Our company was found by Daniel J Chapu. A young entrepeneur with a degree in Software Engineering. He believes in pursuing dreams and chasing them until they are achieved. The company was founded in 2022 in hopes to create and foster an economic system that supports and uplifts local business. We are located in the vibrant and never sleeping city of Harare where many aspiring people seek to seize the moments and opportunities.
